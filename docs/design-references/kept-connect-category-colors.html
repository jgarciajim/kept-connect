Kept Connect · brand · category system

Service families, not per-trade colors.

Color groups related trades; the icon glyph identifies the specific one. Eight families cover every trade in and around a home and stay distinguishable — and any new trade slots into a family instead of needing a new color.

The rule: color = coarse grouping (which family), glyph = the trade. Chips are a neutral surface with the family-colored glyph (the Uber pattern) — so a wall of categories reads calm, never rainbow. Two colors are reserved and never appear here: terracotta (brand/action) and emerald (verified).
